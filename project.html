Project 1


Competency -Design and Development of Learning Systems.

I am able to design and develop innovative, aesthetic, effective and sustainable learning technologies based on theory-grounded research.

For digital media class I created a google site.

The site includes items created around a theme. The theme I chose was my cat, Rummi. Utilizing Adobe Photoshop and Adobe Illustrator I created a pdf for My Time with Rummi. Adobe Premier was used to create my video “Kitty Cocoa Time.” The audio recording was made using Adobe Audio.
I feel these pieces represent a multi-faceted aspect of using digital media effectively within a theme. This experience and techniques learned can be applied to many future projects.
My attention to detail and quality products are a result of hard work.

Project 2


Competency- Front-End Analysis and System Evaluation.

I developed critical and analytical skills for data collection and evaluation and can reflect and apply these skills to assess and evaluate learning systems. MiZoomi was a group project for an Instructional Systems Design Project. MiZoomi was created to teach new educators how to use Zoom for their classrooms. Our project design was set up to provide training for educators that come straight out of industry into the classroom. Many of these educators have 6,000 work experience in a related field to what they are teaching. We created a three day workshop for the training. The instructional design project included Goals, Needs and Task Analysis, Formative and Summative Evaluation, Learner and Contextual Analysis, Types of Learning Experiences, Materials, and Implementation Plan and Schedule.
